Course breakdown
Swim · 1.2 miles
Willamette River
Downstream point-to-point. Fastest 70.3 swim on the circuit. First 200m shallow — do NOT stand. Let the current carry you.
Bike · 56 miles
Wine Country
Flat to rolling · 1,368ft gain · deceptively easy feeling. NP 145-152w absolute max. Conservative bike = strong run.
Run · 13.1 miles
Minto-Brown Park
Two loops · fully shaded paved trail. July heat up to 90F — shade is your advantage. Use every aid station.
Race day strategy
Swim
Start easy, let current work. Do not stand first 200m. Sight every 6-8 strokes. Exit water HR should feel controlled.
Bike
NP 145-152w ceiling. First 20 miles feel easy — that is correct. Eat every 20 min. Drink every 10 min. The run starts at mile 57.
Run
Miles 1-3: hold back. Miles 4-9: 9:00-9:10/mi. Miles 10-13: whatever you have. Walk 15 sec at aid stations if needed.
